Welcome to Grouse Mountain

Grouse Mountain is one of Vancouver's most popular outdoor destinations. With breathtaking views of the city, hiking trails, wildlife encounters, and skiing in the winter, there is always something exciting to do. Whether you're an adrenaline seeker or simply looking for a peaceful retreat in the mountains, Grouse Mountain has something for everyone.

Grouse Mountain Views

Enjoy panoramic views of Vancouver, the Pacific Ocean, and surrounding mountains from the summit of Grouse Mountain. It's the perfect spot for photos and taking in the beauty of nature.

Mountain Coaster

Take a thrilling ride down the mountain with the Grouse Mountain Coaster. A fun and exciting experience for the whole family.

Skyride

Soar above the trees with the Skyride gondola, which provides spectacular views as it ascends to the summit of Grouse Mountain.

Things to Do at Grouse Mountain

There are plenty of activities to enjoy at Grouse Mountain all year round:

  • Hike the Grouse Grind
  • Enjoy the Wildlife Refuge
  • Go skiing or snowboarding in winter
  • Take the Skyride gondola
  • Experience the Mountain Coaster
