Welcome to Capilano Suspension Bridge

The Capilano Suspension Bridge, located in North Vancouver, offers breathtaking views of the surrounding rainforest and the Capilano River below. This iconic bridge is 137 meters long and 70 meters above the forest floor, providing an exhilarating experience in the heart of nature.

Suspension Bridge

Cross the famous Capilano Suspension Bridge and take in stunning views of the surrounding rainforest and river below.

Treetop Adventure

Walk among the trees on a series of suspended bridges high above the forest floor, offering unique perspectives of the canopy.

Cliffwalk

Experience the cliffside walk with glass walkways that hug the granite cliffs, offering awe-inspiring views of the forest and canyon below.
