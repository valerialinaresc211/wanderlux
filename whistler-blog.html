Welcome to Whistler

Whistler is a world-renowned mountain resort located in British Columbia, Canada. Known for its stunning natural beauty, Whistler is the perfect destination for outdoor enthusiasts and those looking to unwind amidst breathtaking scenery.

Skiing & Snowboarding

Whistler's mountains offer some of the best skiing and snowboarding slopes in the world. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, there's a slope for everyone to enjoy.

Summer Adventures

In the summer, Whistler transforms into a hub for hiking, mountain biking, and zip-lining. Explore miles of scenic trails or enjoy the views from the Peak 2 Peak gondola.

Whistler Village

Whistler Village is the heart of the resort, offering a variety of shops, restaurants, and entertainment. After a day of adventure, unwind in this charming pedestrian village.
