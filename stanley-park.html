Welcome to Stanley Park

Stanley Park is a 1,000-acre park located in Vancouver, British Columbia. With lush greenery, a scenic Seawall, and a rich history, it's one of Vancouver's top attractions. Whether you're here for a relaxing walk, a bike ride, or a chance to immerse yourself in nature, Stanley Park has something for everyone.

Stanley Park Seawall

The Seawall offers stunning views of the Pacific Ocean, the North Shore mountains, and Vancouver's skyline. It's perfect for walking, biking, or just relaxing by the water.

Lost Lagoon

This peaceful freshwater lagoon is a great spot for bird watching, canoeing, or a quiet walk through nature. It's also home to many species of wildlife.

Totem Poles

Stanley Park is home to the largest collection of totem poles in the world, showcasing indigenous art and culture of the Pacific Northwest.

Things to Do in Stanley Park

Whether you're an outdoor adventurer or a lover of culture, Stanley Park offers a variety of activities and attractions:

  • Bicycle along the Seawall
  • Visit the Vancouver Aquarium
  • Explore the forest trails
  • Admire the Totem Poles
  • Relax at Second Beach or Third Beach
